# FAQ & Disclaimer

## Frequently asked

**Do I need an account?**\
No — the whole terminal is usable signed out. Signing in adds cloud sync + [Rewards](../track/rewards.md).

**Why does a token say "no backbone coverage"?**\
It isn't in the scored universe yet (the composite needs indexed history). The **live on-chain tools still
work** — use the Forensics / Wallet Cluster / On-Chain
tabs. See [Token Workspace](../research/token-workspace.md).

**Which chains are supported?**\
Solana, Ethereum, BSC, Base, Arbitrum, Optimism, Polygon, Avalanche — coverage varies per tool. On-chain
**forensics & clustering** run on EVM chains (incl. BSC) and Solana.

**My Wallet Cluster scan is slow.**\
A full Pro scan is an async job (top-holder cohort → multi-layer funder trace → economics). It can take
1–3 minutes; you'll see a progress bar.

**How do holder-change alerts reach me?**\
Telegram (message the alerts bot first, or use your numeric chat-id) or email (stored & queued — delivery
pending an email provider). See [Wallet Cluster](../research/wallet-cluster.md).

**Is the Narrative feed a price predictor?**\
No. It's a rules-based **same-day context** read; a 360-day backtest showed near-coin-flip forward
accuracy. See [Narrative](../discover/narrative.md).

**Are the trades real?**\
No — [Signal-Trade](../track/signal-trade.md) trades are **paper / simulated** for transparency
and research.

**Are points worth money?**\
No. Points are an **engagement score** — not a token, security, or guaranteed reward.
