# Getting Started

This page gets you from "first open" to "I found what I needed" in a couple of minutes.

## 1 · Open the terminal

Go to **[vntgai.com](https://vntgai.com)**. No install, no
sign-up required to look around — the full terminal is usable immediately.

## 2 · Take the guided demo

On your first visit a **guided interactive demo** starts automatically. It *drives the terminal itself*
— navigating through each module and loading real sample data while a floating card explains what you're
looking at.

* **Next / Back** — step through at your pace.
* **Arrow keys** — ← / → also navigate; **Esc** closes.
* **❔ button** (top bar) — replay the demo any time.
* **"Don't show again"** — stops it auto-starting on future visits.

{% hint style="success" %}
The demo is the fastest way to understand the layout. Let it run once end-to-end.
{% endhint %}

## 3 · The layout

The left sidebar has three groups:

| Group | What it's for |
| --- | --- |
| **Research** | Work **one token** in depth — search a contract, get a verdict, drill into tabs. |
| **Discover** | Browse the **whole market** — Radar, Flow, Narrative, Degen Lab. |
| **Track** | The **public record** (Signal-Trade), your **saved tokens**, and your **Rewards**. |

The top bar has: the **theme toggle**, the **❔ demo** button, a **points badge** (once signed in), and
**Sign in**.

## 4 · Research your first token

1. Open **Research → Token**.
2. Paste a **contract address** — `0x…` (EVM) or a Solana base58 address — or type a **ticker**.
3. Press **Analyze**.

You'll get an instant **composite verdict** and a plain-English read, then you can drill into the tabs:
**Perpetual · On-Chain · Wallet Cluster · Forensics**. See [Token Workspace](research/token-workspace.md).

{% hint style="info" %}
**Early / unlisted token?** Use **[Degen Lab](discover/degen-lab.md)** — it scans brand-new contracts
that aren't in the scored universe yet, then lets you jump straight into Forensics and Wallet Cluster.
{% endhint %}

## 5 · Sign in (optional, recommended)

Signing in with **Google** or a **wallet** unlocks cloud sync (your bookmarks & history follow you) and
**Rewards** (points, referrals, leaderboard). See [Account & Sign-in](account.md).
